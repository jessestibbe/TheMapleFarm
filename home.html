The Maple Farm


About Our Farm

In 1973 we opened our maple farm in norther Quebec, tapping our own trees on the property and boiling down our delisous syrup into the classic maple syrup can you see today.


The Process

  1. Tap the maple trees and place a bucket to collect.
  2. Once the buckets are full bring them to our shop and boil them down.
  3. Once the syrup is thick and has changed to the golden brown colour can the syrup.
